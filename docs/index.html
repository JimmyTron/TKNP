Introduction

Student’s Clearance Portal is intended to help create efficiency, effectiveness of operations, cost effectiveness and it is also aimed explicitly at providing security and integrity of information related to all transactions by elimination of unauthorized access by the personnel to the records of transactions that have taken place.

Description

The clearance process is divided into two major categories:

  • Departmental Clearance - Form 2103

  • General Clearance - Form 2102

The two are further sub-divied into two parts each.

Departmental Clearance

Form 2103 A - Departmental Clearance

clear with all subject/unit tutors/lecturers. The number varies with number of units taken in the specified year of study. My suggestion is all this functions be done by the Head of department since He/she keeps the departmental records of students and to prevent bias.

Form 2103 B - Departmental Liability

Generally filled by the Head of Department of the department in cases where the student owes the Department.
